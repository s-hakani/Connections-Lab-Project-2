~ take one, give one ~

The prayers that are auto-populated come from a specific container created in October 2021: 10 people from Sarah's family together under one roof, mapping to the 10 dots above, saying goodbye to her grandmother who moved back to India. While the project intent is to be a prayer container for queer people of faith, the container of the multigenerational home in flux serves as a blueprint for what it looks like to house disparate but connected prayers.

Welcome! We are glad to have you here. This is a container of the wishes, dreams, goals, and desires of queer people of faith around the world. Our goal is that this space provides you community, comfort, and peace knowing that someone is wishing you well and thinking of your prayer.

We hope that you share a prayer and receive one, and make sure to check out the prayer gallery to see our full archive.
